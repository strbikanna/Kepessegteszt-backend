Coglica Application

Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which the Publisher collects, uses, maintains and discloses information collected from users in the Coglica Application (later: the Application). Please read our privacy policy carefully to get a clear understanding of how we collect, use, protect or otherwise handle your Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with our website.

The application is a companion application for “MTA-AVKF Tanulási Környezet Kutatócsoport” and it is used to involve people in the research.

What personal information do we collect in the app?

In order to use the application, users must authenticate themselves with an email address and their name. Providing your real name is not mandatory and is never checked.

The application estimates the registered user's partial abilities based on their performance in the game-based tests, using ability models applied in research to generate cognitive profiles from different perspectives. These estimates are not necessarily accurate, as the primary goal of the application is to develop new cognitive ability models.

How is the personal information used?

The data collected by the application is used by the research team members to support studies aimed at enhancing the learning experience through statistical analysis, machine learning, or other methods. The estimated cognitive ability profiles can be organized into groups (e.g., schools, classes) to create various aggregates. Researchers never share personal data with third parties without your permission.

What other anonymous information are collected?

We use Google Analytics in the application to anonymously understand how users use the application and to improve it in the future.

How do we protect visitor information?

Our backend is scanned on a regular basis for security holes and known vulnerabilities in order to keep it as safe as possible.

We use regular Malware Scanning.

The backend of the system does not collect any personal information from the end users.
